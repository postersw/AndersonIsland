7/26/16. NEW FERRY TICKETING SYSTEM:
The Anderson Island Assistant now has 3 buttons to link to the new ferry ticketing system. First, tap 'FERRY'.
The "BUY" button takes you to the web site to buy tickets.
The "ANDROID" button takes you to the Google Play store to install the Android ticketing app; if the app is already installed, click on 'OPEN' to open the app.
The "iPHONE" button takes you to the Apple App store to install the iPhone ticketing app; if the app is already installed, click on 'OPEN' to open the app.
