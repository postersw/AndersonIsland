About Us

The Anderson Island Assistant is a handy little mobile app whose purpose is to provide instant access to all sorts of good Anderson Island stuff. It is available now as a web-based app from www.anderson-island.org, as well as an Android phone app on Google Play. It will be available soon for iPhones on the iPhone App Store, and Windows phones at the Windows Store.

The Anderson Island Assistant is created and produced by island resident Bob Bedoll at Poster Software, LLC. (www.postersw.com).
