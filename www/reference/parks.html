PARKS

The Anderson Island Park District currently supports six developed and three undeveloped parks
All parks are open from 7 a.m. to 10:30 p.m. year-round unless otherwise posted.
Andy's Marine Park
81 acres. 3/4 mile public beach and tidal inlet. 1 mile trail (through woods) to the beach. (Some steep parts.)
Facilities: Restroom at trailhead only.
Location: Trailhead on Clausen Road.
Andy's Wildlife Park
180 acres wedlands, tidal estuary, and forest. 2 mile nature trail loop. Best in summer due to wet conditions.
Facilities: Restroom and picnic area at trailhead.
Location: trailhead at intersection of Eckenstam-Johnson and Sandberg Rd.
The Old School House
Historic one-room schoolhouse and tennis/pickleball/basketball court. The schoolhouse serves as a fitness center run by Wendy Field 884-9261. Facilities: portable restroom. Location: Intersection of Eckenstam-Johnson Rd. and 108th St.
Jane Cammon Park
40 acres. Includes Elementary School, ballfield, covered play area, play equipment, and the Jane Cammon Walking and Bicycle Trail (2 miles flat and dry). Location: Camus Rd. between 130th Ave and Burton Rd.
Montalvo Park (Undeveloped)
5 acres. Wooded with open area with picnic table. Location: Steffensen Rd.
