PARKS

The Anderson Island Park District supports six developed and three undeveloped parks
All parks are open from 7 a.m. to 10:30 p.m. year-round unless otherwise posted.
Andy's Marine Park
81 acres. 3/4 mile public beach and tidal inlet. 1 mile trail (through woods) to the beach. (Some steep parts.)
Facilities: Restroom at trailhead only.
Location: Trailhead on Clausen Road.
Andy's Wildlife Park
180 acres wedlands, tidal estuary, and forest. 2 mile nature trail loop. Best in summer due to wet conditions.
Facilities: Restroom and picnic area at trailhead.
Location: trailhead at intersection of Eckenstam-Johnson and Sandberg Rd.
Jane Cammon Park
40 acres. Includes Elementary School, ballfield, covered play area, play equipment, and the Jane Cammon Walking and Bicycle Trail (2 miles flat and dry).
Location: Camus Rd. between 130th Ave and Burton Rd.
Jacob's Point Park
82 acres of woods, wetlands, and beach. Historically a pioneer farm. 1.4 mile (each way) trail. Composting toilet near trail end. Location: Eckenstam-Johnson Rd.
Lowell Johnson Park / Ol' Swimming Hole
2 swimming areas and sandy beach on Lake Florence. Floating Dock and slide. Volleyball court. Picnic area.
Location: Guthrie Rd.
The Old School House
Historic one-room schoolhouse and tennis/pickleball/basketball court. The schoolhouse serves as a fitness center run by Wendy Field 884-9261.
Facilities: portable restroom.
Location: Intersection of Eckenstam-Johnson Rd. and 108th St.
Tom White Park
5 acres. Wooded area with hiking trail and a salmon creek.
Location: Ekenstam-Johnson Rd across from the Old School House.
