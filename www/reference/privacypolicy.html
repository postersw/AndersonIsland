PRIVACY POLICY of the ANDERSON ISLAND ASSISTANT

This application collects the following personal data:
  • Location - your current location is used to highlight either the Steilacoom or Anderson Island ferry schedule row.
  • Calendar - the app accesses your calendar if you request to schedule an event, activity, or ferry run on your personal calendar.
  • Usage - the app collects anonymous usage data for statistical purposes only. This includes the number of times that the app is used, and the app pages that are visited. This data is aggregated to show what parts of the app are used.

    • No identification, location, or calendar information is kept on the Anderson Island web site. No information is disclosed to third parties.
