Anderson Island Assistant News


7/20/18 FERRY NOTIFICATIONS:
Beginning 9/1/18, versions of this app older than version 1.19 will no longer receive ferry notifications. If your app is not at version 1.19 or newer, please update it by going to the Apple App Store for iPhones, or the Google Play Store for Android phones, and searching for Anderson Island Assistant. The App Version can be found at the bottom of the main screen (the first screen) for the App. (With version 1.19 in June 2018 we changed the service provider that send notifications from 'Pushbots' to 'OneSignal' - the old 'Pushbots' service will be turned off on 9/1/18.)
4/26/17. CHURCHES:
A list of Anderson Island churches, and their service times, has been added under ISLAND INFORMATION LINKS -> CHURCHES.
4/26/17. CALENDAR:
The calendar on the Anderson Island Assistant lists ALL public events on the island. If an event is missing, please let us know through the 'Feedback' link.
