Anderson Island Association and Island Sounder Newsletter

Anderson Island Association

The Anderson Island Association ("AIA") was formed in 1981 with an original charter to address law enforcement and safety issues as an interface with Pierce County The AIA has since evolved into a communication entity and sponsor of other island activities, such as the AIGS bulletin board, summer ice cream social, island clean-up, and the Christmas Star Lighting.

The AIA publishes the Island Sounder, a monthly newsletter that includes upcoming island events, news, and advertisements for companies doing business on Anderson Island.

Island Sounder

The Island Sounder is prepared and distributed by the Anderson Island Association as a communication vehicle for Anderson Island. We welcome and encourage reports from the various island organizations and comments on a wide range of subjects related to the island.

To submit material for the Sounder: email all submissions to islandsounder@gmail.com. Guidelines for submission are available from the editor. The deadline for submission is 5pm on the 15th of the month before publication.
