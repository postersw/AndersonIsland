Anderson Island Assistant

If you are an Anderson Island (Washington) resident or visitor, this little app is a 'must-have'. It will give you instant access to a wealth of up-to-the-minute Anderson Island information. And it's fast, small, and ad-free.

Hi, I'm Bob Bedoll, Anderson Island resident and software developer. I've put this little app together to make Island life a little easier for all of us. I hope you give it a try and find it as useful as I have. Thanks.

Information includes:
    The current effective ferry schedule (adjusted for day-of-week and holidays), ferry-line webcams, and the current ferry position.
    The current and forecast tides.
    The current and forecast weather.
    The upcoming island events and activities.
    The current hours of operation of island businesses.
    The burn-ban status,.
    The Tanner electrical outage status.
    Island emergency contacts.
    Links to additional and more detailed island information.
 All the information is automatically updated and is always current.

iPhone/iPad users: Install the free iPhone/iPad app from the Apple App Store.

Android phone users: Install the free Android phone app from Google Play.

PCs, tablets, or other phones: Point your web browser to: http://www.anderson-island.org.
