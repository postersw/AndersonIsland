Anderson Island Assistant Info

If you are an Anderson Island (Washington) resident or visitor, this app is a 'must-have'. It will give you instant access to a wealth of up-to-the-minute Anderson Island information. This includes:
  • The current effective ferry schedule (adjusted for day-of-week and holidays), ferry-line webcams, and the current ferry position.
  • The current and forecast tides.
  • The current and forecast weather.
  • The upcoming island events and activities.
  • The current hours of operation of island businesses.
  • The burn-ban status,.
  • The Tanner electrical outage status.
  • Island emergency contacts.
  • Links to additional and more detailed island information.
All the information is automatically updated and is always current.
