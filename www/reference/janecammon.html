Jane Cammon Park

HIKING, BALLFIELD.

FACILITIES: 40 Acres.
  • The 1 mile loop trail in this park offers a year-round hiking opportunity as it lacks wet spots and standing water that hikers in other Anderson Island Parks frequently experience.
  • There is also a challenging off-road bicycle course.
  • This park also includes the Anderson Island Elementary School
  • Russ Cammon Ballfield
  • A covered play area, and play equipment.
LOCATION:
Located on Camus Road between 130th Ave. and Burton Road.
