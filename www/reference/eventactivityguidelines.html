EVENT and ACTIVITY POSTING GUIDELINES

These guidelines are used to decide whether an event or activity will be shown in the Anderson Island Assistant Event and Activity schedules.
  • The event or activity must be open to all or a significant percentage of island residents.
  • The event or activity must occur in an Island public space, such as a park, the school, the Community Center, the AIHS farm, a room at the Rivera, or one of the churches. (Events in private houses will not be listed).
