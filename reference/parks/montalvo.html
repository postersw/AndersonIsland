Montalvo Park

HIKING, PICNIC TABLE

FACILITIES:
Five wooded acres with an open area for Frisbee throwing or Badminton with a picnic table. A trail connects Montalvo Park with the Jane Cammon Trail.

LOCATION:
Located off of Steffensen Road.


HISTORY:
Montalvo Park was a 2009 gift from Steve Montalvo in memory of his parents.
