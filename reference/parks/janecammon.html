Jane Cammon Park

HIKING, BALLFIELD.

FACILITIES: 40 Acres.
  • The 1 mile loop trail in this park offers a flat, year-round hiking opportunity as it lacks wet spots and standing water.
  • There is also a challenging off-road bicycle course.
  • A covered play area, and play equipment, ajacent to the school.
  • Portable toilets near school play field and in the 97th Street parking lot
  • This park also includes the Anderson Island Elementary School and Russ Cammon Ballfield
  • A 1/2 mile trail (at the NW corner) connects to Montalvo park.
DOGS:
Leashed dogs allowed on trails (Jane Cammon only, NOT connecting trail to Montalvo).

LOCATION:
Parking and trailhead available from Anderson Island Elementary (on Camus Road between 130th Ave. & Burton Road).
Second trailhead and parking available from the end of 97th St Ct.


HISTORY:
The property was donated by Pierce County in 1968.
