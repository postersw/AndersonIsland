SERVICE SIGNUP

To signup for a listing on the Anderson Island Assistant 'Local Services' page, simply fill out this form and submit it. Your listing will appear within 48 hours.

Some Simple Rules:

  • These listings are to help residents of Anderson Island. You or your business must be located on Anderson Island (have an AI address).
  • There is no charge for the listing.
  • Listings will expire after one year. You will be sent an email asking if you want to list for the next year. This is to keep the listings current.
  • Listings are moderated by Poster Software, LLC, the publisher of the Anderson Island Assistant.
  • All listings will be identical in format.

All fields are required.
