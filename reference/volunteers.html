Anderson Island Volunteer Opportunities

The following organizations are always in need of volunteer help.

AIHS - Anderson Island Historical Society

  • Help with any of the many events. Contact Jeanne McGoldrick 884-4096.
  • Weekly farm maintenance work parties. Just show up Wednesday morning at the Johnson Farm. Or call Wayne Wallace 884-0843.
  • Become a docent: guide visitors through the farm. Contact Peggy Hodge 884-2078.
  • Help at the gift shop on Saturday and Sunday. Contact Kathy Bailey 884-2135.

AICC - Anderson Island Community Club

If you would like to volunteer for any of our events or fundraisers, contact Lynda Baile at 884-0717.

Volunteer Island Patrol

Reduce crime and increase safety and security on the island.

Anderson Island Fire and Rescue - PCFD27

Anderson Island Fire/Rescue is always looking for people who are interested in becoming part of a special team. Enjoy a rewarding experience and make new friendships, all while serving the community you live in and protecting your neighbors. No experience is necessary… and all volunteers will receive the equipment and training required for the job at no cost to you. Our volunteer firefighters provide fire and EMS services to people on the island 24/7. We would love for you to become a part of our team. Please call or visit the station for more information and to get your volunteer application.
