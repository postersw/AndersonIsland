To find your purchased tickets on the City Experiences ticket app:
  1. After you have bought tickets, find the 3 small gray bars in the upper left hand corner of the map on the Pierce County Ferry page in the app. (right hand corner for iPhones).
  2. Tap on those bars and it will show a menu.
  3. Tap on 'Use Tickets' and it will show you your tickets.
  4. Tap on a ticket to expose the QR code for scanning.
